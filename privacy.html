FilmDice Privacy Policy

Last updated: 18 June 2026

FilmDice does not run its own servers and does not collect your personal data for its own use. There is no account to create with us, no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking across other apps or websites. Almost everything stays on your device; the few things that leave it go only to services you control or to anonymous lookups, as described below.

Approximate location

If you allow it, FilmDice uses your approximate location (rounded to about 11 km) to look up current weather, so the dice can suggest a film stock that suits the light. Your coordinates are sent only to the weather service (Open-Meteo) to fulfil that request and are not stored by us or linked to your identity. The feature is optional, and the app works without it.

Your photos

When you turn on “Save developed rolls to Photos,” FilmDice asks for access to your photo library so it can create and add to a “FilmDice” album. It uses this access only to save the photos you develop in the app and to manage that album. It does not read, scan, upload, or transmit the rest of your photo library, and nothing from your library is collected by us or sent off your device. If you have iCloud Photos enabled, Apple syncs the saved photos to your own iCloud automatically.

Frame.io (Adobe) and your camera

FilmDice connects to your own Frame.io account (provided by Adobe) so your camera’s photos can reach the app over Frame.io’s Camera-to-Cloud feature, where they are downloaded to your device to be “developed.” These photos travel through your own Frame.io account, not through any FilmDice server. Your use of Frame.io is governed by Adobe’s privacy policy. FilmDice also connects to your camera over Bluetooth and USB to help load film recipes; this does not collect any personal data.

What we don’t do

  • No analytics or usage tracking.
  • No advertising and no ad identifiers.
  • No tracking of you across other apps or websites.
  • No selling or sharing of personal data. We don’t collect it to begin with.

Children

FilmDice is rated 4+ and does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new date.
