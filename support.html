What you need to use FilmDice

  • A Fujifilm camera that supports Camera-to-Cloud (Frame.io).
  • A free Frame.io account, connected in the app's Setup.
  • Your camera's clock set correctly, so frames are matched to rolls by capture time.

Common questions

  • It says “offline” or “reconnect.” Your shots are safe. The camera uploads to Frame.io on its own, so frames are never lost. Reconnect when you're back online and they'll sync into the right roll.
  • My photos didn't appear in an album. Turn on “Save developed rolls to Photos” in Settings and grant Full Access when asked; developed rolls then save to a “FilmDice” album.
  • Can I shoot offline? Yes. Shoot away; the app counts frames by hand and pulls the photos once you're back online.
