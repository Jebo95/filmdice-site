FilmDice

It's film, on the camera you already own.

Roll the dice for a film stock, dial it into your Fujifilm, and shoot the roll blind. No chimping, no instant review. Develop it later in the app and finally see what you caught.

Coming to the App Store

How it works

1

Roll the dice

Get a film stock for the day, with its box speed and any push baked in.

2

Dial it in

Set the recipe on your Fujifilm and load a fresh roll in the app.

3

Shoot it blind

Frames count themselves over Camera to Cloud. No previews until you develop.

4

Develop

Open the darkroom, develop the roll, and see every frame at once.

What you need

  • A Fujifilm camera that supports Camera-to-Cloud (Frame.io).
  • A free Frame.io account, connected in the app's setup.
  • Your camera's clock set correctly, so frames match rolls by capture time.

Your photos stay yours

FilmDice has no servers of its own. There is no account to create with us, no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking. Your photos travel through your own Frame.io account and live on your device. Read the full privacy policy.
